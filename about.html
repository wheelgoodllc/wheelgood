Our Story

Part bike shop, part time machine. We believe the best gear is the gear that gets you out the door.

Where Retro Meets What's Next

Wheel Good Bicycle Repair Co. started with a simple idea: bikes are timeless technology, and they deserve a shop that treats them that way. We grew up wrenching on hand-me-down ten-speeds and dreaming about the bikes of tomorrow — so we built a shop that lives in both worlds.

Today we service everything from vintage steel frames to the latest e-bikes, with the same care and straight-talk pricing either way.

What We Believe

Every bike deserves an honest mechanic, a fair price, and a shop that explains what it's doing and why.
